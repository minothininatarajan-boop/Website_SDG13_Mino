Climate Action Hackathon 2025

About the Event

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 focuses on addressing urban heat and flood resilience challenges in cities worldwide. This event brings together passionate students, developers, designers, and community innovators to co-create innovative digital solutions that directly contribute to mitigating climate risks in urban environments.

We aim to foster collaboration and innovation, leveraging technology to build prototypes, applications, and data visualization tools that can empower communities. Our participants will engage in activities designed to drive the creation of green infrastructure, early warning systems, and climate risk awareness initiatives.

SDG 13: Climate Action

SDG 13 focuses on urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. This hackathon aligns with the global goal by fostering solutions that help mitigate urban heat and improve flood resilience. Participants will develop solutions to reduce climate risks, with an emphasis on community-level interventions, data-driven tools, and digital solutions that contribute to achieving SDG 13.

Through collaboration and creativity, the goal is to create prototypes that can directly help communities face the growing challenges of climate change, specifically urban heat islands and flooding.
